  % Questions
    \instr{
      Have students form into small groups.
      Each group is to be given an inventory of allophones with some of those allophones marked as non-phonemic.
      The task of each group is to then invent phonological rules that would yield the non-phonemic allophones and to then invent 20 words using this inventory as well as a name for the language.
      Some of the words they invent should include the context(s) where the phonological rules they created apply and some should not.
      After finishing their list of words, they will hand that list (with the language name) off to another group who will then have to determine which phonological rule(s) are involved in the invented language and write out those rules correctly.

      Here are some allophone inventories that can be used:
